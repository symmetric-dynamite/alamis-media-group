Frequently Asked Questions

These are answers to some of the most common questions out clients ask.

  • What services do you provide?

    We are a full service agency. We can handle your campaign from conception to production, or handle tasks within your campaign. It is entirely up to you.

  • I already have my own design. I just need help finding a space. Do you charge us upfront?

    No. We do not charge clients upfront. Our commission is collected after the deal has been made.

  • Why are your prices so much lower than the competition?

    We simply operate on lower profit margins, choosing to focus on economies of scale. Our partners have also contractually agreed to numerous discounts.

  • What if you make a design for me and I'm not happy with it. Do I have to pay?

    For design work we do have to charge you 50% upfront. But we do offer unlimited revisions for free. Once you are satisfied with the work, the remaining 50% is due.

  • I'm not really sure what to do or what my options are. Is it possible to have a free consultation?

    Yes. We don't charge for consultations. Our staff will be happy to meet with you. Learn more about your needs. And create a proposal.
